Born : December 27, 1965

Early Life :
Salman Khan is the son of legendary screen writer Salim Khan. His brothers Sohail and Arbaaz are actors who produce and direct films as well. Salman started his career with a supporting role in the movie Biwi Ho To Aisi (1988). His first on screen appearance as a lead actor was in Maine Pyar Kiya (1989). The film was a runaway success and Salman was touted as one of the most promising newcomers that year.

Career Highlights :
Following his smashing debut, Salman appeared in films like Baaghi (1990), Saajan (1991), Andaz Apna Apna (1994) and Hum Aapke Hain Koun..! (1994) which made him a formidable force to reckon with. Other noteworthy performances included blockbuster hits like Karan Arjun (1995), Jeet (1996) Judwaa (1997), Pyaar Kiya To Darna Kya (1998), Biwi No. 1 (1999), Kuch Kuch Hota Hai (1999) and Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam (1999). In 2003, he appeared in Tere Naam which received substantial accolades from the critics as well as the masses. Salman has always served as a role model and inspired a generation of youngsters to lead a healthy lifestyle. He has enjoyed a string of successful hit films, notably Wanted (2009), Dabangg (2010), Ready (2011), Ek Tha Tiger (2012), Dabangg 2 (2013), Jai Ho (2014) and Kick (2014). Apart from acting, he is extremely popular on the small screen as well. He hosted two seasons of Dus Ka Dum and 4 seasons of the controversial reality show Bigg Boss. A man of the masses, Salman is known for his action movies, a global fan base and humanitarian endeavours. He is an active philanthropist and has set up his own charitable trust, Being Human. In 2011, he launched his own production company, Salman Khan Being Human Productions (SKBH Productions). He was last seen in Kick (2014) and is currently working on Prem Ratan Dhan Payo.
